## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1. Client Management Solutions (CRM Dashboard)
* **Objective:** Create a centralized hub for effective client management that empowers agencies to streamline workflows and enhance engagement.
* **Key Features:**
  * Designed a dark-themed interface with tools to optimize lead tracking and visualize the lead pipeline clearly.
  * Incorporated data visualization elements to provide a comprehensive view of client profiles, engagement metrics, and task reminders.
  * Structured customizable workspace widgets to prioritize assignments and leverage automation for task management.

### 2. Wellness & Salon Mobile App
* **Objective:** Design a minimalist, user-friendly mobile application that simplifies the booking experience for wellness and beauty services.
* **Key Features:**
  * Crafted a calming color palette and intuitive interface allowing users to effortlessly browse various categories and view specialist profiles.
  * Streamlined the booking calendar feature to ensure users can quickly find and secure ideal appointment times that fit their schedules.
  * Designed clear confirmation screens and dedicated sections for discovering tailored, rejuvenating massage and skin treatments.

### 3. Pollachi Pizza Ordering Interface
* **Objective:** Develop an interface that drives immediate orders and reservations by providing a user-friendly experience that celebrates local culinary heritage.
* **Key Features:**
  * Emphasized community connection and high-quality, locally sourced ingredients through vibrant, warm visual design elements.
  * Engineered a frictionless, three-step ordering process: choosing authentic local favorites, customizing toppings, and placing the order or booking a table.

## 🧠 Design Process & Learnings
1. **Empathize & Define:** Conducted research to understand diverse user pain points, whether for agency teams managing leads or local customers ordering food.
2. **Ideate & Wireframe:** Established solid layout hierarchies and user flows before applying visual aesthetics.
3. **Design & Prototype:** Built high-fidelity, interactive prototypes focusing on modern aesthetics, accessibility, and clear calls to action.
4. **Develop & Hand-off:** Bridged the gap between design and engineering by translating visual components into responsive HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
